## Overview

House cleaners and janitorial service operators who clean residential and small commercial properties. Shares the Contractor Daily engine with vocabulary and form template adaptations. Room-based checklists replace task narratives. Before/after photos are the primary client trust signal.

## What This Archetype Covers

Residential house cleaning, move-in/move-out cleaning, recurring maid service, small office/commercial janitorial, deep cleaning services, post-construction cleanup.

## Additional Form Fields (Beyond Core Engine)

- Room checklist (kitchen, bathrooms, bedrooms, living areas, etc.)
- Per-room condition notes (stains, damage, items out of place)
- Supply usage tracking (no cost tracking needed — supplies are overhead)
- Time in / time out per property
- Special instructions completed (client-specific preferences)
- Before/after photos by room
- Key/access notes (alarm codes, lockbox, pet instructions)

## Community Pass Connection

- **Gathering Circle:** Move-in welcome packages, community cleanup events
