## Overview

Independent auto mechanics, mobile mechanics, and small shop operators who repair and maintain vehicles. Shares the Contractor Daily engine with vocabulary and form template adaptations. Adds parts tracking and vehicle history as the client-facing view.

## What This Archetype Covers

Independent auto repair shops, mobile mechanics, specialty repair (brakes, transmission, electrical), fleet maintenance, pre-purchase inspections.

## Additional Form Fields (Beyond Core Engine)

- Vehicle info (year, make, model, VIN, odometer reading)
- Parts catalog with part numbers
- Diagnostic trouble codes (DTCs)
- Warranty status on parts and labor
- Next service recommended (maintenance schedule)
- Before/after photos of repair areas

## Community Pass Connection

- **Creative Alliance:** "Basic Car Maintenance" workshops for community members
- **Gathering Circle:** Car care clinics, community mechanic days
