## Overview

Landscapers and lawn care operators who maintain properties on recurring schedules. Shares the Contractor Daily engine with vocabulary and form template adaptations. Adds seasonal care tracking and property history as the client-facing view.

## What This Archetype Covers

Lawn mowing services, garden design and installation, landscape maintenance, irrigation management, seasonal cleanup, hardscaping, edible garden installs.

## Additional Form Fields (Beyond Core Engine)

- Seasonal care notes (spring prep, fall cleanup, winter protection)
- Irrigation system status / notes
- Plant health observations
- Before/after photo emphasis (seasonal comparison over time)
- Weather impact on scheduled service

## Community Pass Connection

- **Harvest Network:** Edible garden installs, community garden partnerships
- **Gathering Circle:** Neighborhood beautification events, volunteer planting days
