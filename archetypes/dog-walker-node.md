## Overview

Dog walkers and pet sitters who visit client homes to care for animals. Shares the Contractor Daily engine with vocabulary and form template adaptations. The client-facing "Pet Journal" — photos of their pet being cared for — is the primary trust and retention driver.

## What This Archetype Covers

Dog walking services, pet sitting (in-home), pet drop-in visits, puppy socialization walks, special needs pet care.

## Additional Form Fields (Beyond Core Engine)

- Walk duration and route (GPS if available)
- Feeding completed (yes/no, what/how much)
- Water refreshed
- Medication administered (with dosage notes)
- Behavioral observations (energy level, mood, interactions)
- Bathroom notes (relevant for puppy training clients)
- Visit proof photos (pet being walked, played with, resting)

## Community Pass Connection

- **Recess Network:** Active outdoor services, movement-aligned
- **Gathering Circle:** Dog socialization meetups, pet parent community events

## Organism Signals

| Activity | Signal | Level |
|---|---|---|
| Visit logs completed | Business vitality | Business |
| Pet photos shared | Trust through transparency | Relationship |
| Behavioral notes maintained | Professional care quality | Business |
| Pet parent engagement (notes/replies) | Living relationship | Relationship |
| Walkers active daily | Local employment + pet welfare | Community |
